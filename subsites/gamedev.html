GAME DEVELOPMENT

Out of all of the things that I do, my main passion is making games!

I started doing it in 2019 and since then, my goal was to create something amazing one day. Currently, I'm working on "There Is No Fangame: Full Experience" (Fangame of "There Is No Game: Wrong Dimension") and also an unannounced standalone game. Will it soon see the light of day? Only time will tell!

My games were first developed using Construct 2, but in 2023 I switched over to Construct 3.

I'm the leader of a group called Bit Studio and together, we are attending various game jams. Some of our most noticable entries were: Raccon Zoom, You Are Not You and Tech Heaven. I have an opportunity to collaborate with some of the most talented people that I have ever met!

Since Game Development is my main passion, I usually put it over every other aspect of my interests. Which means, that nowadays, you might see me less, doing something else other than working on my games. But it's not always like that!
