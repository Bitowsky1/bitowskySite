2023 is almost over!




What a crazy year, right? For some people, this year was getting very long. For some - it wooshed pretty quickly. For me, though? It was just perfect! Especially because of the new skills I have gained throughout this year. I learned a lot about programming, music creation and 2D animation as well! I also took more focus for vector art, whether it’s Brawl Stars related or anything besides that. It was a year, when I could discover my true skills. And I’m very excited to discover even more of them!

But, I would lie if I said that I achieved that alone. I would be more than wrong! There were a lot of people that I had the pleasure to meet this year. Some of them have been with me for even longer! They have shown their own amazing talents that I could watch infinitely or that I could just hang out casually. The person that I am, wouldn’t be the same without you all, who has been supporting my work all of the time! I’m very grateful for that!

That’s why I have prepared this blog to point out some of the most supportive people that I had the pleasure to meet with this year (or I knew longer). While I got a lot of support, those people were a rain of kind words and overall just cool people I could hang out with. Without them, I don’t know where I would be right now!
